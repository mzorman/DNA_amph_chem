## Description of files in this folder ##
## these are the structures used to generate the pre-assembled tubesA ##
- Files starting with R denote R12 stereochemistry
- Files starting with SR denote (RS)_6 stereochemistry
- Files with 4 or 6 in the middle denote that the given stereocenter(s) has been flipped
- Files labeled curve_topCand are those used as monomeric units
